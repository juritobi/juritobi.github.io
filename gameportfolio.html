Game Programming Portfolio

RYM: Alfred's revenge

C game for Amstrad CPC

Hobby project made with a colleague, it took us 5 weeks to develop and was presented to the CPCRetrodev contest, qualifying 12th out of 49 games.

The game is an action platformer, where the player has to find 4 specific rooms to open the gate to the final boss, we were trying to make a game similar to rogue legacy, with the obvious limitation of an Amstrad CPC.

I was significantly involved in:

  • Movement, combat and collision systems
  • AI design and programming
  • Code structure and optimization
  • Project management
  • Game design, art, music and SFX

Illyria

C++ Game for PC

University project made with 5 other colleagues, programmed exclusively with C++ and OpenGL

The game is a 1 vs 1 arena action competitive game, similar to laser league, you'll have to kill the opponent by activating the traps scattered through the arena.

I was significantly involved in:

  • Game engine structure and optimization (ECS)
  • movement, physics and collision systems
  • AI design, decision making (behavior trees) and execution
  • Project Management
  • Character art, animations, game design, branding, video editor and social networks.

Ratones y mazmorras

Assembly game for Amstrad CPC

University project made with 2 other colleagues, it took us 5 weeks to develop and was presented to the CPCRetrodev contest, qualifying 8th out of 35 games.

The game is a maze action game, where the player has to get the keys to open the door and advance to the next level, we wanted to make something similar to Pacman, but with a level-based design.

I was significantly involved in:

  • AI design and programming

  • Project Management
